Punycode Alerter

What is punycode?

Punycode is a way of representing URLs that allows more characters than ASCII.

Why is this a problem?

An attacker can use punycode to register a domain that looks like an official one. In a browser's URL field, https://www.xn--80ak6aa92e.com looks just like https://www.apple.com. Users can misled into entering their credentials on this fake website.
